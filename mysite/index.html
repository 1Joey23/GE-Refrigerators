★★★★☆

Great!

I'm mostly impressed with my GE refrigerator. It looks fantastic, and the storage capacity is ample for my needs. The adjustable shelves are a nice touch, allowing flexibility in organizing items. My only gripe is that the ice maker can be a bit noisy at times. It's not a dealbreaker, but I wish it were a bit quieter. ★★★☆☆

Decent

The GE refrigerator has its merits, but I've encountered a couple of inconveniences. The design is appealing, and it functions adequately. However, I've noticed that the water dispenser occasionally drips after use, leading to small puddles. Additionally, the freezer compartment tends to accumulate frost more quickly than I anticipated, requiring frequent defrosting. While it serves its purpose, these issues prevent me from giving it a higher rating.
